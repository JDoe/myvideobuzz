Enable Developer mode

Looks like developer mode is still not enabled. Did you complete below steps and did "enable installer" screen show up? If not, Please restart your Roku and try this step again and try to press the below sequence roku remote buttons faster (press remote buttons at the same speed as shown in this video how to enable developer mode or this video).

You need to enter the special remote control sequence to enable development mode. Turn on your TV and use your Roku remote (point towards Roku) and enter below sequence buttons. (Here is a video of how to enable developer mode)

Home, Home, Home, Up, Up, Right, Left, Right, Left, Right

(Important: Try to push the buttons at normal speed without any pause in beetween. Below is remote image for your convenience)

  1. Once you enter above sequence you should see a screen on Roku prompting to "enable installer". Click Ok to enable installer.
  2. Please read the "Developer Agreement" and select "Agree" if you want to proceed (This step is not required in old Roku devices).
  3. Select "OK" to restart your Roku.
  4. Once your Roku restarts click "next" button below.
